Grüezi! I am a biostatistician based in Zurich, Switzerland, working as a Research Associate in the Department of Biostatistics at the University of Zurich. My research sits at the intersection of statistical methodology, clinical trials, and meta-research, with a unifying focus on improving how scientific evidence is generated and evaluated.

I also contribute to the Swiss Reproducibility Network and am a fellow of the Center for Reproducible Science and Research Synthesis at UZH.

Feel free to get in touch if you have questions or want to collaborate.
